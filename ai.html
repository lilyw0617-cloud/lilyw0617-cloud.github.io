IT Innovation (1) — How AI Supports Korean Learning

Using AI tools (similar to ChatGPT) as a 24/7 learning companion:

  • Real-time role-play: ordering food, asking directions, self-introductions
  • Pronunciation feedback: identifies batchim errors and tone issues
  • Generates culturally relevant example sentences (e.g., K-drama style dialogue)

Benefits: Available anytime, low-pressure practice, fully personalized learning
