Technology to Learn Korean Language & Culture

Group members: Wong Ming Lai · Yu Ying Ling

Learn Korean with Fun & Culture!

An interactive web platform that combines Korean language learning with cultural immersion through AI-powered conversations, pronunciation feedback, and cultural content.

Learning Challenges / Problem Statement

  • Hangul is easy to read, but pronunciation is very difficult to master (e.g., batchim, tense/lax consonants)
  • Memorizing words without cultural context makes them easy to forget
  • Lack of interaction leads to shallow understanding of Korean culture (K-pop, K-dramas, food, etiquette)
  • Traditional learning methods feel boring and lack immediate feedback

Target users: Absolute beginners or elementary learners who want to learn Korean and experience the culture in a fun, technology-driven way

Our Solution: K-Culture Connect

A comprehensive Korean learning platform featuring:

  • AI-Powered Conversations: Practice speaking with AI characters in cultural scenarios
  • Pronunciation Feedback: Real-time audio analysis for accurate Korean pronunciation
  • Cultural Immersion: Interactive content about K-pop, K-dramas, food, and etiquette
  • Gamified Learning: Points, badges, and progress tracking to keep learners motivated
  • QR Code Integration: Connect physical learning materials with digital experiences

Working Prototype Features:

  • Simulated QR scanning (via link input) → displays Korean word and cultural info
  • Basic AI chat interface (or link to external AI service)
  • Clean and friendly interface design

Transform boring memorization into an engaging, immersive Korean learning experience.
Increase motivation, improve long-term retention, and deepen cultural understanding.

  • Educational Value: Makes Korean learning accessible and enjoyable for beginners
