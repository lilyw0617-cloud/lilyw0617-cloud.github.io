Learning Challenges / Problem Statement

  • Hangul is easy to read, but pronunciation is very difficult to master (e.g., batchim, tense/lax consonants)
  • Memorizing words without cultural context makes them easy to forget
  • Lack of interaction leads to shallow understanding of Korean culture (K-pop, K-dramas, food, etiquette)
  • Traditional learning methods feel boring and lack immediate feedback

Target users: Absolute beginners or elementary learners who want to learn Korean and experience the culture in a fun, technology-driven way
