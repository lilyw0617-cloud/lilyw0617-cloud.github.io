IT Innovation (2) — QR Codes & IoT

  • Example: Scan a kimchi photo → “김치” pronunciation, making video, etiquette notes
  • Example: Scan a hanok image → “한옥” vocabulary and historical explanation
  • Turns ordinary objects into interactive learning triggers
